Invisalign Treatment Services

We offer customized Invisalign aligner solutions—including Express, Lite, and Full treatments—to correct mild to complex alignment issues with clear, comfortable, and effective care.

1. Invisalign Express

For very minor corrections Small spacing or slight crowding Short treatment (often ~3–6 months)

2. Invisalign Lite

Mild to moderate issues Fewer aligners than full treatment Usually ~6–8 months

3. Invisalign Full (Comprehensive)

The most common option Handles complex cases (crowding, bite issues, rotations) Treatment can last 12–24 month
